VAST 2017 Mini-Challenge 3 Sample

A demostration of dc.js and crossfilter.js, showing the average brightness across sections of the VAST MC 3 data, after applying various filters. The idea is to be able to see clearly where the most
drastic changes took place between March 2014 and December 2016. The available filters are Plant-Health, RGB, Floods-Burned-Land, Moisture-Differentiation, and NDVI.
